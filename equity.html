What this can and cannot show

This is the start of an open question the Bloomberg Harvard report left unanswered: is Memphis 311 service distribution equitable? These averages include all request types, weighted naturally by volume. "Still open" means the city has not yet closed the ticket. Differences across zips are suggestive, not causal: request mix, distance from depots, and contractor zones all matter. Download the raw data (link on About) to reproduce.
